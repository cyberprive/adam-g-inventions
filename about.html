About Adam G

Inventor. Problem-Solver. Patent Holder.

Adam G. Glickfield is the founder of ASG Products LLC and the inventor behind a growing portfolio of patented designs spanning fitness equipment, wearable technology, consumer accessories, and lifestyle products.

With more than a dozen patents and registered trademarks to his name, Adam's work reflects a consistent approach: identify everyday problems that people accept as normal, then design practical, efficient solutions that make life better.

Each invention in the ASG Products portfolio began as a personal observation — a workout that could be improved, a garment that wouldn't stay in place, a pair of expensive shoes being ruined on a concrete walk. From these starting points, Adam developed, prototyped, and patented designs that are now ready for licensing and commercial partnerships.

Adam is based in Miami Beach, Florida, where he continues to develop new inventions and pursue licensing opportunities with manufacturers and distributors worldwide.
