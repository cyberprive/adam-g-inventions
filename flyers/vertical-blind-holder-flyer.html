Vertical Blind Holder (VBH)

Vertical Blind Stabilization System

Key Features

  • Pulls vertical blind slats firmly together for complete light blocking
  • Two-part system (main unit + receiving unit) for secure stabilization
  • No modification to existing blinds required — installs in minutes
  • Creates a clean, uniform appearance when blinds are closed
  • Reduces noise from swinging slats in drafts or air conditioning
  • Easy installation with no tools or expertise required
