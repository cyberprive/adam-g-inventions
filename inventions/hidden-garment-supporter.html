Hidden Garment Supporter

A discreet, adjustable accessory that secures and organizes clothing for a better fit while staying invisible and comfortable under any outfit.

The Innovation

The Hidden Garment Supporter addresses a common but often overlooked problem: keeping clothing properly positioned and fitted throughout the day. Whether dealing with shirts that won't stay tucked, waistbands that shift, or garments that lose their shape during wear, this invention provides an invisible solution.

Protected by two design patents covering different buckle mechanism configurations, the Hidden Garment Supporter features an adjustable strap system with a proprietary buckle design. It can be worn under any outfit, providing secure garment support without being visible or uncomfortable.

The two patented designs offer flexibility for different body types and clothing styles, with both a buckled configuration for secure hold and a released configuration for quick on/off.

Features & Benefits

Completely Invisible

Designed to be worn under any outfit without visible lines or bulk.

Adjustable Buckle System

Proprietary buckle mechanism allows for customized fit and tension.

Universal Compatibility

Works with shirts, blouses, pants, and a variety of garment types.

Two Design Variations

Two patented configurations for different body types and preferences.

Comfortable All-Day Wear

Lightweight materials and ergonomic design for extended use.
