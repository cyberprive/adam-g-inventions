Personal Assistant Glasses

Smart eyewear that merges style and photography with voice-activated digital assistance — keeping you connected wherever you go.

The Innovation

The Personal Assistant Glasses represent the convergence of everyday eyewear and smart technology. Designed for people who want hands-free digital assistance without the bulk of traditional smart glasses, these glasses integrate a computing device directly into the frame.

With two granted design patents protecting the distinctive appearance and a recently approved utility patent (Application #18/352,684) covering the integrated computing device functionality, this invention is positioned at the forefront of wearable technology.

The glasses maintain a sleek, conventional appearance while housing electronics that enable voice-activated assistance, photography, and seamless digital connectivity. The design prioritizes style alongside function — you wouldn't know they're smart glasses just by looking at them.

Features & Benefits

Integrated Computing
