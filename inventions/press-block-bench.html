Press® Block & Press® Bench

Compact modular fitness tools that replace bulky gym equipment — adjustable for presses, dips, step workouts, and full-body strength training.

The Innovation

The Press Block and Press Bench were born from a simple need: a better way to perform bodyweight exercises at home without bulky, expensive equipment. Traditional push-up bars offer limited range of motion and put strain on the wrists. Stacking books or improvised platforms is unstable and unsafe.

The Press Block solves this with a stackable, trapezoidal design that spreads weight evenly across the entire hand for maximum comfort. Its interlocking platform system allows users to adjust the height for different exercises — from push-ups and inverted shoulder presses to dips and step workouts.

The companion Press Bench extends the system into a flat bench for seated exercises, dumbbell work, and cardio stepping — all in a single, portable, space-saving package that replaces push-up bars, a flat bench, a stepper, and parallette bars.

Features & Benefits

Replaces 4 Pieces

One system replaces push-up bars, a flat bench, a stepper, and parallette bars — saving space and money.

Adjustable Height

Stackable platform design allows users to increase or decrease height for customized workout intensity.

Greater Range of Motion

Elevated platform enables deeper push-ups and shoulder presses compared to floor exercises or standard push-up bars.

Compact & Portable

Lightweight, stackable design with built-in handles makes it easy to store, carry, and use anywhere.

Full-Body Versatility

Supports push-ups, shoulder presses, dips, step workouts, crunches, seated curls, triceps extensions, and more.

Ergonomic Comfort

Soft hand-rest surface spreads weight across the entire palm, reducing wrist strain during exercises.
