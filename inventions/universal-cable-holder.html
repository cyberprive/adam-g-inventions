Universal Cable Holder

A sleek, patent-protected organizer that secures and routes cords neatly in homes, offices, and studios for a clean, clutter-free workspace.

The Innovation

Cable clutter is a universal problem in modern living and working spaces. The Universal Cable Holder addresses this with an elegantly simple design — a compact, quarter-round profile piece that mounts easily and routes cables neatly along surfaces.

Protected by two design patents covering two distinct design variations (Version A and Version B), the Universal Cable Holder offers flexibility for different mounting configurations and cable sizes. The compact form factor (approximately 2 x 1.75 x 0.25 inches) makes it unobtrusive while being effective.

Whether managing charging cables on a nightstand, organizing monitor cables behind a desk, or routing audio cables in a studio, this holder provides a clean, professional solution that can be produced at very low cost.

Features & Benefits

Two Design Versions

Patented Version A and Version B configurations for different cable routing needs.

Compact Profile

Small quarter-round form factor (2" x 1.75" x 0.25") sits unobtrusively on any surface.

Universal Compatibility

Works with charging cables, audio cables, USB cords, and virtually any standard cord.

Easy Installation

Simple screw-mount design for permanent placement on desks, walls, or shelving.

Low Production Cost

Simple design and minimal materials make this highly economical to manufacture at scale.
