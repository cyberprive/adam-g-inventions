U.S. Patent D732,846

Vertical Blind Holder

A simple device that stabilizes vertical blinds, pulling the slats together to block light and create a cleaner appearance.

The Innovation

Vertical blinds are found in millions of homes and offices, yet they share a common frustration: individual slats swing freely, allowing light gaps, creating noise in the wind, and giving an untidy appearance. The Vertical Blind Holder (VBH) is a simple, effective solution to all of these problems.

The VBH consists of a main unit and a receiving unit that work together to stabilize vertical blind slats. When engaged, the system pulls slats together firmly, blocking light completely and creating a clean, uniform appearance. When not in use, the slats operate normally.

With a design that requires no modification to existing blinds and installs in minutes, the VBH is ideal for mass-market production and retail distribution. Its simplicity is its strength — minimal parts, minimal cost, maximum impact.

Features & Benefits

Complete Light Blocking

Pulls slats firmly together to eliminate light gaps for true room darkening.

Two-Part System

Main unit and receiving unit work together for secure, reliable stabilization.

No Modification Required

Installs on existing vertical blinds without drilling, cutting, or permanent changes.

Cleaner Appearance

Creates a uniform, tidy look when blinds are closed.

Reduces Noise

Prevents slats from swinging and clattering in drafts or air conditioning.

Easy Installation

Attaches in minutes with no tools or expertise required.
